poietic-generator-reloaded by Gnuside

Poietic Generator Reloaded is a multiplayer and collaborative art experiment.

Behind the strange name of “Poietic Generator” (PG), there is a collective and very rich social experience for children from 7 to 77 years old. The Poietic Generator simply offers to many participants to draw in real time on the same picture. But not anyhow: the PG minimum rules allows gradually the auto-organisation of a global drawing with tens or hundreds participants, as already shown in earlier experiments…since 1986. Yes you have really well read: 1986, the GP idea is an « old » one! Precisely, it’s a pioneering creation of the « Net Art ». Thanks to you and the new version for mobile phones, we will maybe see thousands of people creating on the same image…
